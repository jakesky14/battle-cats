# Battle Cats

A browser-based Battle Cats–style tower-defense game. Earn money over time, deploy cat units to push down a lane and destroy the enemy base, and spend money to upgrade your income speed.

Play it at: https://jakesky14.github.io/battle-cats/

## Controls
- Click a unit in the dock (or press its number key, 1–9, 0 for the 10th) to deploy it.
- Press **E** (or click "Upgrade Income") to increase your money income rate and wallet cap.
